Werewolf The game

How does the game work?

During daytime you talk about the night or choose to elect a mayor.

At night while the villager sleeps, the rest get to do an action with their role.

To get started The Gamemaster give each player a role(choosen at random)

Roles:

  • Werewolf
  • Hunter
  • Witch
  • Healer
  • Oracle
  • Amor
  • Spy
  • Lone Wolf
  • Villager

Every night the werewolf get to pick a person to kill. If the werewolves do not choose a target, they will not kill.

When the hunter dies he can kill someone else.

The witch has 1 healing and killing potion.

She knows who was killed by the werewolves and can choose to heal them if she has her healing potion.

She can use the healing potion on herself to get additional life.

She can use the killing potion at night to kill anyone.

The healer can heal 1 person every night.

However, Unlike the witch, they do not know who was attacked.

The healer may heal themself once and cannot heal the same person twice.

At the start of the night, The oracle get to check to the role of a player.

Only the oracle gets to know the role.

In the first night, Amor choose 2 players.

These 2 players have to team up to win the game.

The 2 players can only win in one of these scenario :

- Both player are town: All Werewolves and Town has to die.

- One of the player is Werewolf: The Town has to die.

- Both player are Werewolves: All Werewolves and Town has to die.

The spy act like a Werewolf. He get to vote like the other werewolves.

However, he may not reveal or hint any roles.

If the werewolves find out they are spy, they dies and cannot be saved by the witch, if that happens it does not count as a kill and Werewolves can attack another player.

The Lone Wolf is a Werewolf but he only wins if he is the last werewolf alive.

He may not kill his werewolves teammate at night but he can accuse them during daytime.

The villagers do nothing at night.
